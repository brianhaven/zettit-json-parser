PROBLEM STATEMENT
-----------------
Script 04 (Geographic Entity Detector v2) was leaving separator words as artifacts
when removing regional entities from titles.

Example:
- Input: "U.S. And Europe Digital Pathology"
- v2 Output: "And Digital Pathology" (separator artifact remains)
- Expected: "Digital Pathology" (clean topic)

SOLUTION IMPLEMENTED
--------------------
Created enhanced v3 implementation (04_geographic_entity_detector_v3_fixed.py) that:
1. Maintains v2's proven priority-based pattern matching
2. Adds smarter cleanup for separator words ("And", "Plus", "&")
3. Detects when separators connect regional entities
4. Removes separators as cohesive units with regions
